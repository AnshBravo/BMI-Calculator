BMI Guidance

Select a BMI to see tailored primary goals and key tips. The card matching your calculated BMI will be highlighted.

Underweight

Range: < 18.5

Primary Goal: Healthy Weight Gain

  • Eat 5–6 smaller, nutrient-dense meals daily.
  • Add healthy calories via nuts, seeds, avocados, and full-fat dairy.
  • Focus on strength training to build muscle mass rather than just fat.

Healthy Weight

Range: 18.5 – 24.9

Primary Goal: Maintenance

  • Balance calorie intake with energy expenditure.
  • Maintain a varied, plant-forward diet rich in antioxidants.
  • Prioritize consistency in existing sleep and exercise routines.

Overweight

Range: 25.0 – 29.9

Primary Goal: Weight Management

  • Aim for a modest, sustainable weight loss of 0.5–1kg per week.
  • Swap sugary drinks for water or unsweetened tea.
  • Track food intake for a few days to identify hidden liquid calories or oversized portions.

Obese (Class I–III)

Range: ≥ 30.0

Primary Goal: Risk Reduction

  • Seek professional medical guidance for a supervised weight management plan.
  • Target a initial 5–10% weight reduction to significantly lower disease risk.
  • Increase activity gradually, starting with low-impact options like brisk walking or water aerobics.

Health Tips

Nutrition

  • Prioritize whole foods (fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, healthy fats).
  • Reduce ultra-processed foods.
  • Focus on adding nutrients (like fiber and protein) rather than only restricting calories.

Hydration

  • Drink 8–10 glasses of water daily.
  • Have a large glass of water before your morning coffee to kickstart metabolism.

Physical Activity

  • Aim for 150–300 minutes of moderate aerobic activity weekly.
  • Incorporate "movement snacks"—short bursts of activity like taking the stairs or stretching every hour.

Sleep

  • Secure 7–9 hours of quality sleep per night.
  • Establish a "digital sunset"—avoid screens at least one hour before bed to regulate melatonin.

Mental Health

  • Practice daily mindfulness or gratitude exercises.
  • Use "mental anchors" like a quiet morning tea or a short walk to ground yourself during stressful days.

Preventive Care

  • Schedule annual check-ups.
  • Stay up to date with age-appropriate cancer screenings and vaccinations.
